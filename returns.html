Return Policy

Information regarding our return and replacement process for Loha Mati creations.

14-Day Returns

We accept returns for items within 14 days of delivery. Because our pieces are entirely handcrafted, slight variations in texture, color, and finish are inherent signatures of the lost-wax casting process and are not considered defects.

Condition of Returned Items

To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original condition, and safely packed in its original packaging. Any items that arrive damaged due to insufficient return packaging will not be refunded.

How to Initiate a Return

1. Email us at bonjour@lohamati.fr with your order number and reason for return.

2. Our team will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.

3. Securely pack the item and drop it off at the designated carrier location.

Refunds & Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval. The refund will be automatically processed back to your original payment method (e.g. Visa, Apple Pay, Google Pay) within 5-7 business days. Please note that original shipping costs are non-refundable.
